    Welcome to the black & white notes

    The To-Do List App is a simple and user-friendly application designed to help individuals organize and manage their tasks effectively. Developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this app allows users to create, edit, delete, and mark tasks as completed, providing an efficient way to keep track of their daily, weekly, or long-term goals.

    With its intuitive and clean user interface, the To-Do List App offers a seamless experience for users to add new tasks. By entering a task description into the input field and pressing the "Add" button, the task will be instantly appended to the list. This feature enables users to quickly capture their thoughts and commitments.

    Furthermore, the app provides convenient options for task management. Users can easily mark a task as completed by clicking on a checkbox associated with each task. Once a task is completed, it will be visually differentiated from the pending tasks, allowing users to see their progress at a glance. Additionally, users can delete unwanted tasks from the list with a simple click on the delete button associated with each task.

    The To-Do List App emphasizes a responsive design, ensuring that users can access and use the application seamlessly across different devices, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones. Whether at home, in the office, or on the go, users can rely on the app to stay organized and focused on their tasks.

    By employing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the To-Do List App offers a lightweight and efficient solution for task management. It eliminates the need for complex setups or third-party integrations, providing a straightforward and standalone application that can be easily customized or enhanced based on specific user requirements.
